Our Story

Wise Choice Gunsmithing is a family-owned business built on a shared passion for firearms and honest, quality workmanship. What started at the kitchen table has grown into a trusted local shop where every customer is treated like family.

We believe in doing the job right the first time — no shortcuts, no compromises. Whether you need a simple repair or a full custom build, we put the same care and attention into every project.

Thank you for trusting us with your firearms. We look forward to earning your business.
